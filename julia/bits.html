GraphIdx.Bits — Module.

Some bit manipulation routines.

source
GraphIdx.Bits.hyperfloor — Method.
hyperfloor(x)

Heighest power of 2 that is smaller than x.

Example

julia> bitstring(UInt8(123))
"01111011"

julia> bitstring(UInt8(GraphIdx.Bits.hyperfloor(123)))
"01000000"
